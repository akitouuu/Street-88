Today Street77 is one of the leading establishments in the field of fast food. They started their activities on August 28, 2015. The first branch was opened in the NEXT shopping mall. At the initial stage of development, the menu could not boast of great abundance, but a variety of various sandwiches, which at that time were the basis of the menu, were quickly able to win the love of guests. Satisfied with the result, but not intending to stop there, we continued to improve. Just a couple of months later, on October 25, 2015, a new branch was opened in the Samarqand Darvoza shopping center. And only a little more than a year and a half later, on May 14, 2017, our main branch on C1 was opened, a cozy institution where you can spend time with family, friends and a loved one. The changes in the menu were enormous, a lot was removed, something was changed, and even more was added, but the quality and taste, so highly appreciated by our dear visitors, remained unchanged. Trying to please the guests, we are expanding the delivery areas, updating the menu, meeting the brands of world standards. Every ingredient used in our kitchen is carefully controlled to eventually become a delicious dish and decoration of your table! To date, Street77 is ready to please you and your loved ones with delicious burgers with minced beef patties, pollock fillets, crispy breaded chicken, delicious salads, soups, and more recently pizza! And we do not intend to stop there. Thank you for staying with us from day to day, from year to year, let's create our own menu, the wishes of our guests are the basis of our innovations, and someday Street77 will delight you with our favorite dishes on every street in Tashkent!
